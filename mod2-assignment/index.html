Best 3 Tanks of World-war-II

The M4A3 Sherman "Easy-8"

American Tank. More powerfull engines allowing for higher speeds, thicker hull armor, advanced suspensions. Armour shredding 76mm cannon with anti-air support from the infantry units. Were good, but not much effective against German Panzer(IV) or Tiger tanks.

Tiger-1(1942)

Most feared tank of WWII. Invincible thick armour, no tank stood a chance against powerfull 88mm Cannon, It had exceptional maneuverability and speed. Weighing 57 tons, speed of 40kmph and range of about 1000 meters.

Soviet IS-2 heavy Tank

Introduced in 1943, the IS-2 carried a monstrous 122mm(4.8inc) cannon, capable of punching a hole right through the armor of the feared Tiger tank, with sloped frontal armour of 120mm(4.72inc)
